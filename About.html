About Me

Hello, my name is Michael Gurshumov and I am the Developer/designer of this project.The main goal of this project is to revive the knowledge some of the users about the classical Muscle cars or as they used to be called American Muscle because they where produced In America With few exceptions. I would like to point out a disclaimer that this is an educational app and give credit to all the resources used in the website. For instance, the images are real images that users post in order to sell or give recognition to their cars.So I truly hope you enjoy this app for comments or ways of contact me please refer to the contact me section.For more information please visit the external links Thank you and hope you will enjoy this website.
